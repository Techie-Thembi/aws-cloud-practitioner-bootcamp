Cloud computing is computing on demand, cloud services include storage,security, etc.

Infrastructure as a service provides storage and servers for your business,Platform as a service provides your business with the necessary infrastructure, hardware and software, Software as a service manages, maintains and operates your software and the infrastructure it runs on.

With On-Premise you keep and maintain all your hardware, software and infrastructure in-house while with cloud your provider(AWS) is responsible for all your compute needs. 

CapEx is the long-term investment in assets that depreciate overtime, you pay upfront for these assests.
OpEx is short-term and refers to the routine day-to-day expenses, you have no ownership and therefore don't have to pay upfront.
